About Cassie:

This is Cassie. She’s 32 -- about to turn 33 in early-June. Yes, this means she’s a Gemini. If you are wondering if what they say about Gemini’s is true. The short answer is YES! She has many different faces. I say that in a good way -- sometimes. Cassie is an ER nurse. She loves to help save lives, but also I think she is in it for the thrills. She considers herself an adrenaline junkie. When she is not working, she enjoys planning trips, locally and internationally. One of her favorite places to go to is LA because of Disneyland. She has been a season pass holder for a few years now. She enjoys the food, the rides, and ofcourse the entertainment. I would consider her a Disney Fanatic. Cassie is also a world traveler. She plans about two trips a year that are outside America. This website is dedicated to all the traveling that she has done and all of her favorite things.
